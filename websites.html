    My Approach

    Talking with clients about their dreams for the site is the most important part of the process. There are millions of ways to create a website. But there's only one vision of yours. I work to understand your plans and then give you something even better.

    Page speed remains a priority for me. I use GTmetrix to evaluate and troubleshoot page loading speed. If your page doesn't load within 2 seconds, I aim to fix that.
