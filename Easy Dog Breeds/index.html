Welcome to Easy Dog Breeds!

Choosing the right breed for your lifestyle helps ensure your dog will be comfortable and happy at home. Our beginner-friendly website will teach you about the best breeds for new dog owners.

While there are hundreds of breeds available, this site covers some of the more popular breeds that work well with beginners. Don't hesitate to search for other breeds, or adopt a mixed breed dog that steals your heart. Our goal is to offer a few options and teach you the kinds of things to look for as you choose the right dog for you.

Breeds

We offer detailed information on the following breeds:

  • Bichon Frise
  • Cavalier Spaniel
  • Golden Retriever
  • Labrador Retriever
  • Yorkshire Terrier

Our comparison chart offers an overview of the traits of each breed so you can find the right breed for your lifestyle at a glance.
