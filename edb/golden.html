Golden Retriever

Golden Retriever
Breed Size Energy Kid-Friendly Grooming Needs
Golden retriever medium-large high yes moderate

Friendly and active, golden retrievers are known for their beautiful golden hair. Devoted and cheerful, these dogs are eager to please and easy to train. Training is important, since their exuberance can be overwhelming to small children or guests who prefer not to be jumped on. With the right guidance, they will get along wonderfully with the rest of the family.

Due to their high energy, goldens need at least an hour a day of activity. Leave them alone too much, and they may entertain themselves to the detriment of your furniture. They thrive in active families, and will remain devoted to their owners.

Traits of a Golden Retriever

  • Medium-large size: A golden retriever is usually around or just under 2 feet tall.
  • High energy: Goldens do best in active families.
  • Kid friendliness: Goldens get along well with kids, and are sturdy enough not to be fazed by rough play.
  • Moderate grooming needs: A golden is fairly easy to groom. Unfortunately, the shedding and drool may be a bit overwhelming.
