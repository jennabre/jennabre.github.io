Bichon Frise

Bichon Frise
Breed Size Energy Kid-Friendly Grooming Needs
Bichon Frise small moderate yes moderate

Bichon Frise (BEE-shawn FREE-say) dogs are energetic, playful, charming dogs. Curious and cheerful, they get along well with just about anyone. Bichons are trainable, and they love performing for their loved ones.

Bichon Frise dogs can get along well with kids and other dogs. Their small size makes them good city dogs. House training can be a challenge for them, but they integrate well into the family in time. Due to their sociable nature and potential for mischief, they're best with an attentive owner.

Traits of a Bichon

  • Small size: A Bichon is usually 1 foot tall or smaller.
  • Moderate energy: Bichons are playful, and are known for running around in a burst of energy called the "Bichon blitz."
  • Kid friendliness: Bichons get along well with most people. However, rambunctious children may play too rough, so take care.
  • Moderate grooming needs: A Bichon needs daily grooming and occasional professional styling. They don't shed or drool much.
