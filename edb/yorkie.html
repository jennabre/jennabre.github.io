Yorkshire Terrier

Yorkshire Terrier
Breed Size Energy Kid-Friendly Grooming Needs
Yorkshire Terrier small moderate somewhat high

Yorkshire Terriers are intelligent, sprightly dogs. Feisty and affectionate, Yorkies are devoted to their owners. They're eager to explore the world around them, and they enjoy long walks. Their tendency to yap makes them good watchdogs, though they can also get on people's nerves. Yorkies rely on good training to teach them boundaries. Sometimes cuddly and sometimes mischeivous, these capricious dogs are loyal companions.

Yorkies' willful personalities can cause conflicts at home. They tend not to get along with other dogs, and they get nippy with kids who get too enthusiastic. House training can also be a challenge with these stubborn dogs. They can adapt well to apartment life. Thanks to their fearless nature and love of adventure, Yorkies are perfect dogs for active adults.

Traits of a Yorkie

  • Small size: Yorkies are usually around 8 inches tall.
  • Moderate energy: Yorkies need more exercise than one might expect.
  • Kid friendliness: Small children and Yorkies do not get along. Yorkies could be injured by a clumsy child, and Yorkies can nip at kids who don't listen.
  • Minor grooming needs: Yorkies' beautiful coats need a lot of maintenance. They require professional grooming and weekly baths. However, shedding and drooling aren't problems.
