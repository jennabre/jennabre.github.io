Why did you create this monstrosity?

The world is filled with troubles. We all need a good laugh sometimes.

Part of my job is understanding the rules that go into creating something that looks good. It's fun to break those rules from time to time.

One interesting part of this project was deciding which rules to break, and how much. For example, eye-searing colors can give sensitive people headaches, which is not my goal here. I'd like people with and without disabilities and sensitivities to enjoy my site.
