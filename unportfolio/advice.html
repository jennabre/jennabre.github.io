Important advice you need in your life

Design

  • Never limit your color scheme. Use all the colors. All of them.
  • More fonts = better.
  • There's nothing wrong with tiny fonts. Weed out the people who don't care enough to squint and strain their eyes. They just aren't dedicated enough.
  • Sometimes, the cruelest thing you can give a client is exactly what they want.

Prosperity

  • If you want people to buy more stuff from your shop, just link to it more. Use lots of links to different products. People will subconsciously want to buy everything.
  • Some people like to do a "grindset" but it is also inappropriate to grind your bones into dust, so keep that in mind. You need your bones for later.

Life

  • When life gives you lemons, crush them with your bare hands. Show it the results of your workouts. This will intimidate/enamor life into giving you its highest-quality fruit.
  • If you don't understand something, just collect as many numbers about it as you possibly can.
  • The trick for never being ashamed for taking a break is to be utterly shameless. Yeah, maybe I'm a potato sometimes. What are you gonna do about it?

And here's the biggest secret of all...

The meaning of life is variable. Earlier, it was hugs. Now, it's chocolate cake.

Tomorrow, it's your turn to choose the meaning of life, so make it a good one.




The meaning of life has not changed yet. Go make the most of it while you can.
